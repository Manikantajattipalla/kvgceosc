Tech Talk on Commercializing Academic Projects

Our college, KVGCE, proudly hosted a tech talk on 25th November 2024, led by Shreemukha Sullia, Founder and CEO of Buttistore.com. The talk focused on the exciting topic of Commercializing Academic Projects and how students can turn their academic work into real-world business ventures.

Details

On 25th November 2024, KVGCE held an inspiring tech talk led by Shreemukha Sullia, an entrepreneur and visionary in the field of academic commercialization. This event, held at the KVGCE Auditorium, attracted a wide range of students, faculty, and tech enthusiasts eager to learn about the process of taking academic projects from the classroom to the marketplace. The event was organized by KVGCE Open Source Community (KVGCEOSC) and co-powered by Buttistore.com. The session began with an introduction to Shreemukha Sullia, who shared his journey of turning innovative ideas into successful business ventures. As the Founder and CEO of Buttistore.com, he spoke about the importance of commercializing academic projects and how students can leverage their academic knowledge to solve real-world problems while creating entrepreneurial opportunities. Shreemukha emphasized the significance of bridging the gap between theoretical learning and practical business applications. He shared valuable insights into identifying marketable ideas, building a business model, and understanding the commercial potential of academic projects. The session encouraged students to think beyond traditional academic boundaries and explore the world of startups, innovation, and technology commercialization. The event also featured a lively Q&A session, where students asked Shreemukha about various aspects of entrepreneurship and commercialization. His answers provided practical advice on how to navigate challenges and seize opportunities in the world of business.
